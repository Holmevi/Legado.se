Jimmy Legado Holmevi

My name is Jimmy Legado Holmevi and I am a creative man from Sweden. I started a new life since I left my job at Axipto AB in Nybro in Sweden 2023 with suicidal thoughts.

In the breakup of my old life, I sold my houses and everything I owned to be voluntarily homeless. I started to travel the world in search of meaning and purpose. And in the deepest darkness I used psychedelics combined with The Bible and Esoteric Science to find Jesus my Lord, King and Savior that saved my life.

I started to walk with God in peace no matter what tribulations life throws at me. And I continued to research The Bible and Esoteric Science with the intention to help other people that suffers the same darkness that I just had survived myself. That’s why I started to take notes to be able to write my first book about my breakup from my old life as a cornerstone to future books of much deeper esoteric and philosophical character.

Travel

Enjoy my adventures all over the world

Follow me on Facebook and TravelPhotoDice.com.

I made myself homeless to make the whole world my home. And as I travel the world, I really enjoy sharing the beauty of everything that crosses my path.

My hope is that I can inspire others to take the step away from repetitive routines and meaningless patterns. As we all carry our inner own child through life, I think it’s our birthright to live our true purpose aligned with our authentic self. And travel has been the best medicine for me to heal the wounds of my inner child.

Music

Traveling troubadour at your service

Follow my YouTube-channel for my latest music.

As I travel the world, I bring my guitar with me wherever I go. And I really enjoy playing and singing with nice people along the way. But of course I also play and sing by myself in the streets, at hotels and in restaurants.

I play Guitar, Banjo, Piano and I love to Sing of course. My favorite genres are American Folk Music, Country, Bluegrass, Jazz, Blues and more. My greatest sources of inspiration are Pete Seeger, early Bob Dylan, Don McLean, Sierra Ferrell, Bella White, Malcolm Holcombe and more.

Photo

I love to share the beauty of life

Follow me on Facebook and TravelPhotoDice.com.

Once upon a time I had a Photo Studio in Kalmar, and I used to work as a Mingling Photographer every weekend. I also worked as a Freelance Photographer for the local newspaper KalmarPosten for a couple of years. I thought that I was a typical extrovert as a person enjoying this life, combined with a lot of parties and other social activities.

But today I have found my way back home to a more authentic passion for photography. So today I want to give my friends and followers on social media a glimpse of all the wonderful places that I visit as I travel the world alone.

Tech

Programming and Web Development

Since I quit my career in the industry sick and heavily depressed, I decided to reeducate myself in Programming an Web Development. So, I made a great investment to educate myself via the company Techover that gave me the opportunity to start doing what I do today. My initial plan was to find a new job, but in the tech industry. But with my bad experiences of how Axipto AB in Nybro treated me after I got sick and depressed after 10 years of dedicated work, I decided to never work for any employer again. So instead I decided to try to build up something valuable by myself aligned with my own passion and authenticity.

Wisdom

Let Light Defeat Demons

I have been searching my whole life for deeper meanings to understand the mysteries of the generative worlds beyond this shallow and illusory world of our 5 senses. And when everything at last make sense it’s clear that God and The Bible is more real than anything I have experienced in my whole life.

Since I stopped believing and started to know God and The Bible, there is nothing like blind faith anymore. And I think that I know how to help people to make sense of God and Esoteric Science even from an atheistic and scientific perspective. That’s why I want to write books on the topic in the future.

Bingu

What is Bingu all about?

I guess a lot of you who follow me on social media have heard me mention the name Bingu sometimes. And to everyone that doesn’t know what Bingu is about, I will here give you a short introduction.

So let me introduce Bingu, my little Penguin that follows me everywhere I go. The reason why Bingu has become such a big part of my life is that he represents my inner child and authentic self. It all started as a joke between me and a friend that came with Bingu to my place in 2016. And today Bingu is following me along my journey through life, and always reminds me to look on life through a joyful and playful perspective.
